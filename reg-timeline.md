# Regulatory Timeline

> **Last verified:** March 2026. Dates are accurate as of the verification date. Regulatory timelines are subject to legislative amendments, court orders, and rulemaking delays.

Chronological milestones, effective dates, and upcoming deadlines for major privacy regulations and U.S. state comprehensive privacy laws. Organized by regulation for quick reference when answering date-specific questions. For granular state-level details (cure periods, opt-out signal deadlines, specific provision dates), consult FPF's [Key Dates for State Privacy Laws](https://fpf.org/key-dates-tracker/) tracker.

## U.S. State Comprehensive Privacy Laws

As of March 2026, approximately 20 U.S. states have enacted comprehensive consumer privacy laws. This table provides enactment and effective dates. For detailed provision analysis, cure periods, opt-out signal deadlines, and specific provision effective dates, consult FPF's [Key Dates for State Privacy Laws](https://fpf.org/key-dates-tracker/) tracker.

|-------|-----|----------------|-------|
| California | CCPA/CPRA | Jan 1, 2020 / Jan 1, 2023 | CPRA amended CCPA; CPPA enforcement from Jul 2023 |
| Virginia | VCDPA | Jan 1, 2023 | AG enforcement; no private right of action |
| Colorado | CPA | Jul 1, 2023 | AG enforcement; universal opt-out mechanism required Jul 2024 |
| Connecticut | CTDPA | Jul 1, 2023 | AG enforcement; includes children's data provisions |
| Utah | UCPA | Dec 31, 2023 | AG enforcement; narrower consumer rights (no correction right) |

| Montana | MCDPA | Oct 1, 2024 | AG enforcement; small-state threshold adjustments |
